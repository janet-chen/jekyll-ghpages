About me

Janet Chen is the co-founder and VR Outreach Director of AGDA VR, the largest student group for virtual and mixed reality development at UBC. She works closely with students, faculty and industry professionals to create immersive education and research tools with Unity for the HTC Vive, Microsoft HoloLens, Google Cardboard, and more. She is currently the lead developer at the Emerging Media Lab, where she is creating an inclusive community for all VR & AR creators at UBC. If you're interested in immersive design, emerging technologies or STEM outreach, I'd love to talk to you.
